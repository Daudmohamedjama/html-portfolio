My top three movies all the time.

Icerde

"Icerde" is a Turkish television series that revolves around the lives of two brothers
on opposite sides of the law one working as a police officer and the other as a
mafia member, as they navigate through betrayal, loyalty, and family ties.

Game of Drones

"Game of Thrones" is a highly acclaimed fantasy television series based on
George R.R. Martin's "A Song of Ice and Fire" novels known for its intricate plots,
complex characters, and epic battles for power in the fictional continents of Westeros
and Essos.

Money Heist

"Money Heist" is a Spanish television series that follows a group of criminals who
plan and execute heists, led by a mastermind known as "The Professor".
