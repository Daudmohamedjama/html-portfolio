The best movies according to Daud

My top five Movies all the time

1. The Shawshank Redemption (1994) – Drama

A powerful story about hope, friendship, and perseverance inside a prison. Consistently ranked among the best movies ever made.

2. Inception (2010) – Sci-Fi / Thriller

A mind-bending film about entering people’s dreams to steal or plant ideas. Great if you like intelligent action and complex plots.

3. The Dark Knight (2008) – Action / Crime

One of the greatest superhero movies ever, featuring Heath Ledger’s legendary Joker performance.

4. Interstellar (2014) – Sci-Fi / Adventure

A visually stunning space exploration film focused on time, survival, and human emotion.

5. Parasite (2019) – Thriller / Drama

An Oscar-winning masterpiece about class differences, suspenseful and full of unexpected twists.
